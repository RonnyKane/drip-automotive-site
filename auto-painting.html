Professional Paint

Paint Work That Stands Up to Florida's Heat

Florida's sun is brutal on automotive paint. Oxidation, fading, peeling, and chalking are all common here in Lake County — and when you add an accident repair or a custom project into the mix, you need a shop that takes paint seriously.

At The Drip, paint isn't an afterthought — it's our craft. Every vehicle that goes through our paint process is properly prepped: stripped back, primed, blocked, and painted in our fully enclosed downdraft spray booth. That controlled environment is the difference between a paint job that looks great on delivery and one that looks great five years later.

Whether you need a single panel blended to match after a fender-bender, a complete respray to refresh your aging vehicle, or a one-of-a-kind custom finish that turns heads — The Drip at 31701 Progress Road in Leesburg is your shop.

The Paint Process

Surface Preparation

Old paint, rust, and contaminants are removed. Body filler is applied where needed, then blocked perfectly flat before any primer touches the surface.

Primer & Sealing

High-build primer is applied and sanded to 400–600 grit. A sealer coat ensures adhesion and uniform color absorption.

Base Coat Application

Color coats are applied in our climate-controlled downdraft booth, eliminating dust, humidity issues, and temperature inconsistencies.

Clear Coat & Finish

Multiple clear coat passes protect the color and deliver that deep, wet-look gloss. Then compounded and polished to perfection.
