Expert Collision Work

Collision Repair That Goes Beyond the Surface

A collision doesn't just bend metal — it can compromise your vehicle's structural integrity, safety systems, and alignment in ways that aren't visible to the naked eye. At The Drip, we approach every collision repair with a comprehensive inspection that goes far beyond what your adjuster photographs.

Our team at 31701 Progress Road in Leesburg uses professional frame measuring equipment to diagnose even minor structural deviations that could affect how your vehicle handles, how it protects you in a future accident, or how it will hold up over time. We don't just make it look right — we make it right.

Serving Leesburg, Fruitland Park, Tavares, The Villages, Ocala, and throughout Lake County — we've been returning accident-damaged vehicles to their owners in better-than-expected condition for since 2020.

What We Repair

  • Front-end and rear-end collision damage
  • Side impact and T-bone damage
  • Frame and unibody straightening
  • Crumple zone and structural panel replacement
  • Bumper repair and full replacement
  • Fender, hood, door, and quarter panel work
  • Radiator support and core support replacement
  • Wheel well and rocker panel damage
  • Airbag system checks and resets
  • Headlight, taillight, and trim replacement

The Repair Process

1

Thorough Assessment

We do a full visual and structural inspection — not just what's visible, but everything the impact may have affected underneath.

2

Estimate & Approval

You receive a detailed, line-item estimate. If insurance is involved, we handle the adjuster communication and supplement process.

3

Repair & Paint

Structural repairs are completed first, then body panels are aligned, filled, primed, and painted to factory spec in our professional spray booth.

4

Quality Check & Delivery

We do a final review of fit, finish, and function before you get your keys back. Your car should look like the accident never happened.
