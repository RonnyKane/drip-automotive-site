About The Drip

We're a locally owned auto body shop in Leesburg, FL that's been doing right by our customers for since 2020. No corporate overhead, no shortcuts — just skilled hands, honest work, and a shop that genuinely cares about the vehicles we touch.

Our Story

Built on Craftsmanship, Grown on Trust

The Drip Auto Body Paint and Restoration started the way most good businesses do — with someone who was simply better at their craft than anyone else around, and enough of a reputation that people kept showing up. Located at 31701 Progress Road in Leesburg, Florida, we've grown into one of Lake County's most trusted names in auto body and paint work.

Over the years, we've repaired thousands of vehicles — everything from a quick door ding to full frame-off classic restorations that took months. Our shop has evolved, our equipment has gotten better, but the core of what we do hasn't changed: we treat every car like it matters, because to the person who owns it, it does.

We serve Leesburg and the surrounding communities — The Villages, Fruitland Park, Tavares, Mount Dora, Clermont, Ocala, and throughout Central Florida. We've earned the loyalty of multi-generation families, business fleets, car clubs, and collectors. That doesn't happen by accident. It happens by doing good work, being straight with people, and standing behind everything we do.

What We Stand For

How We Do Business

These aren't just words on a website. They're the principles that show up every day in how we treat customers and how we approach every job.

🎯

Honest Estimates

We tell you what the repair actually requires — not a number designed to win the job and then inflate later. No bait-and-switch, no hidden fees. If we find more during the repair, we call you before we do it.

🛠️

Do It Right the First Time

Cutting corners on body and paint work just creates problems down the road — and those problems come back to the shop. We prep properly, use quality materials, and take the time the job deserves.

🤝

We Work for You

When insurance is involved, we advocate for proper repairs — not the cheapest repairs the insurer will approve. We're on your side of the table, not theirs.

🏘️

Community First

We're a Leesburg business. We live here, our customers are our neighbors, and we take that seriously. What we do in our shop reflects on this community — and that keeps us honest.

📞

Real Communication

You'll always know where your vehicle is in the process. If something changes, we call you. If repairs take longer than expected, we tell you before you're waiting in our parking lot.

✅

We Stand Behind Our Work

If something isn't right when you pick up your vehicle, we make it right — no argument, no runaround. The job isn't done until you're satisfied with it.

Our Facility

A Shop Built for Professional Results

Good bodywork starts with the right environment. Our shop at 31701 Progress Road is equipped with the tools and facility required to deliver professional results consistently — not just on good days.

  • Downdraft Spray Booth — climate-controlled, dust-free paint environment for flawless finishes
  • Computerized Color Matching — spectrophotometer technology for exact factory color reproduction
  • Frame Measuring System — precision structural diagnostics on every collision job
  • PDR Tool Suite — professional-grade paintless dent repair equipment and LED lighting boards
  • Full Metal Fab Capability — for restoration rust repair and custom work
  • Detailing Bay — final polish and prep before every vehicle delivery
