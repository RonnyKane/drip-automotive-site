We're in Your Corner

We Handle Your Insurance — Start to Finish

Most people dread the insurance claim process after an accident — and for good reason. It's paperwork, phone calls, adjuster negotiations, and the constant worry that the insurance company will approve less than your car actually needs. At The Drip, we take all of that off your plate.

We've worked with every major insurance carrier operating in Central Florida. We know the process, we know the language, and we know how to document damage so that your vehicle gets repaired to the standard it deserves — not just the minimum the insurer wants to pay.

We handle hail damage claims, collision claims, deer strikes, uninsured motorist claims, and more. If it's covered under your policy, we'll help you get what you're owed.

Our 6-Step Insurance Claim Process

1

Call Us First

Before you call your insurance company, call us. We'll walk you through what to say, what to document, and what to expect. (352) 805-1818.

2

Bring Your Vehicle In

Drop it off at 31701 Progress Road in Leesburg. We photograph and document all damage thoroughly — not just the obvious stuff, but structural and hidden damage too.

3

We Contact Your Insurance

We open the claim on your behalf, submit our detailed estimate, and communicate directly with your insurance company and their assigned adjuster.

4

Adjuster Review & Approval

The adjuster reviews the estimate. If they approve less than the actual scope of repair, we submit supplements with documentation and push back professionally.

5

Repairs Completed

Once approved, we complete all repairs to the highest standard. We don't cut corners to match a low estimate — we negotiate the estimate up to match the proper repairs.

6

Direct Billing — You Pay Only Your Deductible

We bill your insurance directly. You pay only your deductible (if applicable) when you pick up your vehicle. That's it.

Types of Claims

We Handle All Types of Vehicle Claims

🚗

Collision Claims

Rear-end collisions, T-bones, sideswipes, and other accident scenarios. We document all structural and cosmetic damage and submit a complete estimate.

🌩️

Hail & Storm Damage

Comprehensive claims for hail damage, falling debris, and storm-related damage. PDR is often covered — we assess, claim, and repair.

🦌

Animal Strike Claims

Deer, raccoons, and other animals cause significant damage in Lake County. These are typically covered under comprehensive coverage.

🔥

Fire & Flood Damage

Comprehensive coverage usually covers fire, flood, and vandalism. We assess, document, and handle the restoration process from start to finish.

🎯

Uninsured Motorist

If the at-fault driver has no insurance, your own UM coverage kicks in. We help you navigate this more complex claim type with confidence.

📋

Third-Party Claims

When the other driver is at fault, we file against their liability coverage on your behalf — and we fight if the insurer tries to lowball the repair.
